# 🐂 Red Bull On-Premise Sales Analysis & Dashboard

## 📖 Project Overview
This project features a comprehensive sales data analysis and dashboard visualization created as part of the Red Bull On-Premise Sales Job Simulation via Forage[cite: 4]. The analysis evaluates account performance across four key segments—Bar, Club, Hotel, and Restaurant—from 2017 to 2021[cite: 6]. 

The primary objective was to extract actionable business insights regarding revenue distribution, sales growth, and Compound Annual Growth Rate (CAGR) to formulate strategic recommendations for the sales team[cite: 6].

![Red Bull Sales Dashboard](red%20bull%20dashboard.png)

## 🛠️ Tech Stack
* **Data Visualization:** Power BI[cite: 5]
* **Business Intelligence:** KPI Tracking, Trend Analysis, Strategic Forecasting[cite: 6]
* **Domain:** FMCG / On-Premise Sales[cite: 4]

## 📊 Key Business Insights
* **Revenue Leadership:** The Club account generated the highest revenue, contributing $409K (27.59%) to the $1 million total sales portfolio over the five-year period[cite: 6].
* **Growth Engine:** The Restaurant category emerged as the fastest-growing account with a remarkable 41% CAGR, presenting significant opportunities for future investment[cite: 6].
* **Consistent Expansion:** The overall portfolio maintained a strong average CAGR of 37%, reflecting robust market demand and consistent sales growth across all account types[cite: 6].

## 💡 Strategic Recommendations
* **Prioritize Restaurants:** Focus future investments on the Restaurant segment to maximize ROI based on its high growth potential[cite: 6].
* **Defend Club Accounts:** Protect the revenue leadership of the Club segment through targeted loyalty initiatives and upselling opportunities[cite: 6].
* **Data Expansion:** Incorporate regional breakdowns and profitability metrics into future analyses for more informed decision-making and performance tracking[cite: 6].
